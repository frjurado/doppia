Editorial Notes

Tempo & Rubato

The "Raindrop" prelude relies on a persistent A-flat (or G-sharp) pedal point. The Urtext suggests a stricter adherence to the Sostenuto marking than modern romantic interpretations.

Source Material

Based on the autograph in the National Library of Poland and the first French edition (M. Schlesinger, Paris, 1839).
